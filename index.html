Web Development Workshop

2 day web development Workshop is conducted by TinkerHub AEC in association with IEEE AEC on 14,15 of September 2023. In this Workshop students are introduced to the HTML, CSS and Javascript. Workshop is absolutely free and Certificate is worth 15 activity points.

> Participants must complete a basic project within 2 days. Only those who finished projects is eligible for Certificate.
> Participants are requested to bring their own lunch
> Participants are requested to bring their own Laptops. Laptops will be provided to those who can't bring it.
